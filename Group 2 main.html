Introduction:

Max's Group: Usually meets on every other Monday (with a few rare exceptions), lasts around 30 min, and consists of Max (DM), Cole, Wyatt, Matt, and Dylan.


Summary of the Story so far:

Since the unleashing of Adultron, the Crustlands has sustained immense damage, even a centry after the event the fallout of the attack remains. The only hope of continued survival of the land is a group of consistantly fluctuating number of adventurers who may yet cause more damage than they fix. Armed with the abilities of rock, the taming of anything, and to increase and decrease the diameter of a laser, hopefully they will be able to be utterly decimated before they can cause even more harm.
